# Use Subaccounts and Split Settlement

## In a nutshell

The Subscriptions Engine uses tenant account mapping so subscription payments can be associated with the right business. You do not need to build a wallet or balance system to integrate the billing API.

## Payment model

```txt
Customer pays
  -> Engine creates checkout or tokenized-card charge
  -> Tenant account information maps payment to your business
  -> Platform fee is tracked by the platform
  -> Invoice and payment state are updated
  -> Your webhook endpoint receives billing events
```

## What you provide

When your tenant is created, your business is associated with an account or subaccount identifier. The engine uses that mapping when creating Nomba-powered payment requests.

## What webhooks tell you

Use invoice and payment webhooks to reconcile revenue in your own system:

- `invoice.paid`
- `invoice.payment_failed`
- `payment_success`

## Not exposed as public API

Balance, payout, and settlement-reporting endpoints are not currently available in the public API.
